Account & Settings

How do I create an account?
Creating an account is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the prompts. You can register using your email address or connect with your Google, Facebook, or Apple account.
How do I change my account settings?
To modify your account settings:
  • Click on your profile picture in the top right corner
  • Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  • Here you can update your:
    • Profile information
    • Email preferences
    • Privacy settings
    • Reading preferences
    • Notification settings
How do I reset my password?
To reset your password:
  1. Click "Sign In" on the top navigation bar
  2. Select "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password
Note: Reset links expire after 24 hours for security purposes.

Reading Experience

Can I read stories offline?
Yes! Premium members can download unlimited stories for offline reading. Free users can download up to 3 stories per month. To download a story, simply click the download icon on any story page.
How do I customize my reading experience?
StoryHub offers several customization options:
  • Font Settings:
    • Choose from multiple font styles
    • Adjust font size
    • Change line spacing
  • Display Options:
    • Light/Dark/Sepia modes
    • Adjust brightness
    • Change background color
  • Reading Mode:
    • Scrolling or Page-flip
    • Single or double-page layout
    • Reading progress indicators

Writing & Publishing

How do I start writing on StoryHub?
To start writing, click on "Write" in the navigation menu. You can create a new story, set up chapters, and use our rich text editor to bring your story to life. We provide tools for formatting, chapter organization,ory publisstory, hing.
How do I publish my story?
To publish your story on StoryHub:
  1. Click "Write" in the navigation menu
  2. Select "New Story"
  3. Fill in your story details:
    • Title and description
    • Cover image
    • Genre and tags
    • Age rating
  4. Write or upload your chapters
  5. Preview your work
  6. Click "Publish" when ready
Note: All stories must comply with our content guidelines.

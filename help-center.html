Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. You can register using your email address or connect with your Google or Facebook account. Fill in the required information, agree to our terms of service, and you're ready to go. Once registered, you can customize your profile and start exploring stories.

Yes, StoryHub offers a free tier that gives you access to thousands of stories. You can read, comment, and engage with the community without any cost. We also offer a Premium subscription that unlocks additional features like ad-free reading, early access to new chapters, and the ability to download stories for offline reading.

To publish a story, first create an account and verify your email. Then, navigate to your dashboard and click "Create New Story." Fill in the story details like title, description, genre, and cover image. Next, you can start adding chapters to your story. You can save drafts, preview your work, and publish when you're ready. Our author guidelines provide more detailed information about formatting, content policies, and best practices.

If you've forgotten your password, click on "Sign In" and then select "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in your email and follow the instructions to create a new password. For security reasons, the reset link expires after 24 hours.

If you encounter content that violates our community guidelines, you can report it by clicking the three dots (⋮) next to the story or chapter and selecting "Report." Choose the appropriate reason for reporting and provide additional details if needed. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24-48 hours and takes appropriate action according to our policies.
